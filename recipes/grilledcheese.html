Grilled
Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese, a timeless classic, embodies simplicity at its finest. It starts with two slices of buttered bread, golden and crisp on the outside, yet soft and warm within. Nestled between these slices is a generous layer of gooey, melted cheese, tantalizingly stretching with each bite. As the sandwich grills to perfection, the cheese melts into a creamy, savory embrace, while the bread transforms into a golden vessel of comfort. With its humble ingredients and comforting aroma, grilled cheese transcends mere sandwich status to become a cherished comfort food, evoking memories of cozy lunches and childhood nostalgia with every bite.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough
    Bread
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Ham

Steps

    Prepare ingredients
    Begin to toast bread
    Add ham and cheese
    Cook for 5 minutes on both sides
    Let rest, then serve
